    Dodgers’ Corey Seager Shortstop Sidelined

    By THE ASSOCIATED PRESSMARCH 13, 2016

    Continue reading the main story Share This Page
    Continue reading the main story

    Corey Seager, the Los Angeles Dodgers’ rookie shortstop, will be out one to two weeks with a sprained left knee.

    Pain in the knee forced Seager to leave in the third inning of an 8-4 spring training loss to the Angels on Friday.
